# Privacy Policy - CoreJourney

**Last Updated: November 2025**

## Introduction

CoreJourney ("we", "our", or "us") operates the CoreJourney mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Email address** - Used for account creation and authentication
- **Display name** - Optional, for personalization

### Usage Data
- **Training progress** - Days completed, exercise history
- **App usage** - Features used, session duration
- **Device information** - Device type, operating system version

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for:
- **Account Management** - Creating and maintaining your user account
- **Progress Tracking** - Saving your training history and achievements
- **App Functionality** - Providing core features of the App
- **Improvements** - Analyzing usage to improve the App

## Data Storage

### Local Storage
- Training data is stored locally on your device using Isar database
- Data persists between app sessions

### Cloud Storage
- Data is synchronized to Firebase Cloud Firestore
- Enables multi-device access
- Provides backup and recovery

## Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Sell your personal data
- Share your data with third parties for marketing
- Use your data for advertising

We MAY share data:
- With service providers (Firebase, Google Cloud) for app functionality
- If required by law

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your data
- **Delete** your account and data
- **Export** your data
- **Opt-out** of data collection (will limit app functionality)

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
- Firebase Authentication security
- Firestore security rules

## Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Firebase Authentication** - User sign-in
- **Cloud Firestore** - Data storage
- **Firebase Analytics** - Usage statistics (anonymized)

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
